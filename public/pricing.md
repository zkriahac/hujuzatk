# Pricing — Hujuzatk PMS (حجوزاتك)

**Last updated**: 2026-05-28
**Currency**: USD (primary), SAR equivalents shown for Saudi customers
**Trial**: 14 days, no credit card required, full Pro features
**Billing**: Annual (no monthly plan)
**Where to buy**: https://hujuzatk.com/#pricing

---

## Basic

- **Promo price (year-end 2026)**: $35 / year (~131 SAR)
- **Standard price**: $40 / year (~150 SAR)
- **Billing**: Annual
- **Trial**: 14 days free
- **Rooms / units**: up to 50
- **Bookings**: unlimited
- **Users**: 1 owner account
- **Channel sync**: not included (manual booking entry only)
- **Included features**:
  - 5-year booking calendar
  - Automated PDF invoicing
  - Financial analytics and occupancy reports
  - Multi-currency support (SAR, AED, KWD, BHD, QAR, OMR, USD, EUR, TRY)
  - Full Arabic RTL + English UI
  - Installable PWA (works offline, push notifications)
  - Guest management
  - Multi-tenant workspace at hujuzatk.com/{workspace}

## Pro

- **Promo price (year-end 2026)**: $58 / year (~217 SAR)
- **Standard price**: $65 / year (~244 SAR)
- **Billing**: Annual
- **Trial**: 14 days free
- **Rooms / units**: up to 50
- **Bookings**: unlimited
- **Users**: 1 owner account
- **Channel sync**: **included** — automatic two-way iCal sync, scheduled daily
  - Airbnb
  - Gathern (جاثرين)
  - Booking.com
- **Included features**: everything in Basic, plus:
  - Automatic channel sync (Airbnb + Gathern + Booking.com)
  - Orphan-booking cancellation when removed from source channel
  - Channel-coloured badges on booking views

## Frequently asked pricing questions

**Is there a free plan?**
No. There's a 14-day free trial of the full Pro plan, then it's $35–$58/year.

**Is there a monthly billing option?**
No — annual billing only. The annual prices are designed to be affordable for independent hosts (Basic works out to ~$3/month).

**Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro mid-cycle?**
Yes — pay the prorated difference.

**Do you support refunds?**
Within 14 days of payment if you haven't actively used the channel sync.

**Are prices in SAR fixed?**
No — Hujuzatk is billed in USD. SAR equivalents above are converted at ~3.75 SAR / USD and may vary slightly at time of payment.

**Do you offer enterprise / multi-property pricing?**
For more than 50 rooms or multiple separate properties, contact via WhatsApp at https://wa.me/905523205496.
