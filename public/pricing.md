# Pricing — Hujuzatk PMS (حجوزاتك)

**Last updated**: 2026-05-28
**Currency**: USD (primary), SAR equivalents shown for Saudi customers (~3.75 SAR / USD)
**Billing**: Annual (no monthly plan)
**Where to buy**: https://hujuzatk.com/#pricing
**Trial**: 14 days, no credit card required

---

## Trial (Free)

- **Price**: $0
- **Duration**: 14 days
- **Rooms / units**: up to 3
- **Bookings**: unlimited within trial
- **Credit card required**: no
- **Includes**: full calendar, invoicing, reports, Arabic + English UI, PWA install
- **Channel sync**: not included (Pro feature)

## Basic

- **Price**: $40 / year (~150 SAR)
- **Billing**: Annual
- **Rooms / units**: up to 10
- **Bookings**: unlimited
- **Channel sync**: not included
- **Included features**:
  - 5-year booking calendar
  - Automated PDF invoicing
  - Financial analytics and occupancy reports
  - Multi-currency support (SAR, AED, KWD, BHD, QAR, OMR, USD, EUR, TRY)
  - Full Arabic RTL + English UI
  - Installable PWA (works offline, push notifications)
  - Guest management
  - Multi-tenant workspace at hujuzatk.com/{workspace}

## Pro

- **Price**: $90 / year (~338 SAR)
- **Billing**: Annual
- **Rooms / units**: up to 30
- **Bookings**: unlimited
- **Channel sync**: **included** — automatic two-way iCal sync, scheduled daily
  - Airbnb
  - Gathern (جاثرين)
  - Booking.com
- **Included features**: everything in Basic, plus:
  - Automatic channel sync (Airbnb + Gathern + Booking.com)
  - Orphan-booking cancellation when removed from source channel
  - Channel-coloured badges on booking views

## Enterprise

- **Price**: $140 / year (~525 SAR)
- **Billing**: Annual
- **Rooms / units**: unlimited
- **Bookings**: unlimited
- **Channel sync**: included (same as Pro)
- **Included features**: everything in Pro, plus:
  - No room cap
  - Suited for multi-property operators and larger vacation-rental portfolios

## What's NOT included on any plan

- SSO / SAML
- White-labelling

- Shomous integration for licensed hotel establishments (not supported)

## Frequently asked pricing questions

**Is there a free plan?**
No permanent free plan. There's a 14-day free trial (Trial tier, 3 rooms), then it's $40–$140/year depending on tier.

**Is there a monthly billing option?**
No — annual billing only. The annual prices work out to ~$3.30/month (Basic) up to ~$11.70/month (Enterprise).

**Can I upgrade between tiers mid-cycle?**
Yes — pay the prorated difference.

**Do you support refunds?**
Within 14 days of payment if you haven't actively used channel sync.

**Are SAR prices fixed?**
No — Hujuzatk bills in USD. SAR equivalents above are converted at ~3.75 SAR / USD and may vary slightly at time of payment.

**Do you offer custom enterprise pricing for 100+ rooms?**
The published Enterprise plan ($140/yr, unlimited rooms) is the top tier. For more complex multi-property setups, contact via WhatsApp at https://wa.me/905523205496.
